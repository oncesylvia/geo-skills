# Converting & attributing AI traffic

## How the AI visitor differs from a search click

| | Search click | AI-referred visitor |
|---|---|---|
| Knowledge on arrival | low — still researching | **pre-informed** (AI summarized you) |
| Volume | higher | lower |
| Intent / qualification | mixed | **high** |
| Landing page | often homepage | often the **deep page** that got cited |
| Next step they want | learn | **confirm + act** |

Optimize for **conversion rate of a qualified few**, not volume.

## The conversion checklist (per cited / AI-referred page)

- [ ] **Continues the AI answer** — doesn't restart the pitch from zero.
- [ ] The specific claim AI made is **visible and substantiated** here (no
      bait-and-switch — mismatch kills trust and corrupts your AI rep).
- [ ] **One clear primary CTA** appropriate to the page's intent (trial, demo,
      quote, buy).
- [ ] **Low-friction capture** for the not-yet-ready (email, short form).
- [ ] Proof near the CTA (numbers, logos, testimonial, the comparison).
- [ ] Fast, clean, mobile-fine; CTA above the fold on the cited page.
- [ ] For transactional intent: a machine-readable next action for **agents**
      (→ `agent-ready`).

Common failure: the page that gets cited is a top-of-funnel blog/“vs”/glossary
page with **no CTA at all**. Add one.

## Attribution (prove the ROI)

- Segment AI referrers as a channel: `chatgpt.com` / `chat.openai.com`,
  `perplexity.ai`, `gemini.google.com`, `claude.ai`, `copilot.microsoft.com`,
  and China engines.
- Track per channel: sessions → signups/leads → revenue.
- Tie conversions back to the **cited page / prompt** that drove them (loop with
  `geo-measure` + `prompt-coverage`).
- Note: AI assistants often *influence without a click* (the user reads the answer
  and comes direct/branded later) — watch branded/direct lift too, not just
  referral clicks. Don't over-claim attribution you can't prove.

## Feed it back

Invest in the prompts/pages that **convert**, not just the ones that get cited.
A page cited a lot but converting nobody is a conversion problem, not a GEO win.
