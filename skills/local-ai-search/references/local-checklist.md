# Local AI search — checklist

## Google Business Profile (the anchor)
- [ ] Claimed and **verified**.
- [ ] Primary + secondary **categories** correct.
- [ ] Hours accurate (incl. holidays); not wrongly marked closed.
- [ ] Address / service area correct; phone + website right.
- [ ] Description, attributes, **photos**, products/services filled in.
- [ ] Posts used; **Q&A** seeded and monitored; **reviews answered**.

## NAP consistency (name / address / phone)
- [ ] Identical NAP on: your site, GBP, Apple Maps, Bing Places, social, every
      directory.
- [ ] No old addresses/phones lingering on stale listings.
- [ ] One canonical format (suite, abbreviations) used everywhere.
- Mismatches split your authority and confuse engines about which location is
  real — this is the highest-leverage local fix.

## Schema & pages
- [ ] `LocalBusiness` (or specific subtype) schema: name, address, `geo`, hours,
      phone, `sameAs`, priceRange.
- [ ] A genuine **page per location / service area** (not thin doorway pages).
- [ ] Embedded map + directions on location pages.

## Listings & reviews
- [ ] Listed accurately on Apple Maps, Bing Places, and **industry-specific**
      directories (Yelp, TripAdvisor, niche).
- [ ] Steady flow of **real reviews**; recent; responded to.
- [ ] Never buy, incentivize dishonestly, or fake reviews — fraud + suspension risk.

## Local prompts to spot-check (per engine)
- "best <service> near me" · "<service> in <city>" · "<service> open now near me"
- "top-rated <category> <neighborhood>"
Record who's cited and what source (often GBP, Yelp, a local roundup).

## 中文 / 国内
- [ ] **高德地图 / 百度地图**:认领门店,信息完整准确。
- [ ] **大众点评 / 美团**:认领,真实点评积累与回复。
- [ ] NAP(名称/地址/电话)在各平台一致。
- [ ] 国内本地搜索与海外 GBP 各做一套。
